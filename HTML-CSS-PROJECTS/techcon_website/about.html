History

Techcon was established in 2010 by Tech Gaint and Entrepreneur
Thabang Kgatlhanye, with the intention to bring together all great minds in tech from the youth to the veterans.

Techcon 2010

Since its inception, Techcon has grown to become
the most anticipated Tech Event and has managed to produce world leaders and
come up with solutions to solving the worlds grand challenges.

Mission

Our mission is to solve the worlds grand challenges,
by leveraging Tech minds to collaborate and grow a network of problem-solvers.
